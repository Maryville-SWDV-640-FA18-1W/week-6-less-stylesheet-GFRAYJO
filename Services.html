Our Services



Our Licensed Trained Professionals are here to support YOU in the times of need

Some of those services include: Individual and family counseling, Peer Support Services, and other types of miscellaneous services







Counseling Services

All counseling services are provided by licensed trained professionals who are here to help you.

Peer Support Services

All of our Peer Support Services are group sessions with others who have faced some of the
challenges as you and could help provide support and assistance on overcoming those challenges

Miscellaneous Services

We offer various types of services on a first come first serve basis as our partners find availability
within their schedules. Many of our miscellaneous services will include: dental cleanings, free giveaways
of hygiene products, free haircuts, and more!
